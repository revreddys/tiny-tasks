# Privacy Policy for Tiny Tasks

**Last Updated: November 28, 2024**

## Introduction

Tiny Tasks ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address** - Used for parent account authentication
- **Password** - Securely stored using Firebase Authentication (encrypted, never stored in plain text)

### Child Profile Information
- **Child's first name** - Used to personalize the app experience
- **Avatar selection** - Visual customization choices
- **Task completion data** - Stars earned, streaks, badges achieved

### App Usage Data
- Daily task completion records
- Reward redemption history
- App preferences (theme settings)

## How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:
- Authenticate parent accounts
- Track children's task progress and achievements
- Award stars, badges, and maintain streaks
- Enable the rewards system
- Improve app functionality

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely using **Google Firebase** (Firestore database)
- Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- PIN codes for profile protection are **hashed using SHA-256** before storage
- We implement Firebase Security Rules to protect data access

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Tiny Tasks is designed for use by families with children. We take children's privacy seriously:

- **We do not collect personal information directly from children** - Parents create and manage child profiles
- **No advertising** - We do not display ads to children
- **No third-party tracking** - We do not share data with advertisers
- **Parental control** - Parents have full control over child profiles and can delete them at any time

## Data Sharing

We **do not sell, trade, or share** your personal information with third parties, except:
- With **Google Firebase** for data storage and authentication (see [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy))
- If required by law or legal process

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us
- Child profile data can be deleted by parents within the app

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
